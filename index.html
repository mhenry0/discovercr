Discover Costa Rica and its Natural Treasures


Costa Rica is a rich land in natural resources with a top world number of endemic species of flora and fauna. Just imagine that Costa Rica's landmass accounts for only 0.03% of the entire planet's surface. This country is home of approximately 5% of the species worldwide and it is listed among the 20 richest countries of the world in a statistical diversity.

Besides, Costa Rica has 12 main national parks which you can visit to discover a lot of animal species and a wider number of flora species. Down here you can find information of each national park on TripAdvisor, there you can find hotels, restaurants, rent-a-car, and more information. It is advised to reach out to SINAC (Sistema Nacional de Areas de Conservacion) in Costa Rica. They are the public organization in charge of managing the national parks.
